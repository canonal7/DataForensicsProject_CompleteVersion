Our Products

EuCanna offers a variety of what are almost impossible to find highly medicinal products including skin creams, ointments, bath melts, soaps, suppositories and many more. All of these products have been infused with our finest Rick Simpson oil and many of our patients swear by the results.

Goal and Vision

EuCanna began after finding inspiration in the work of Rick SImpson. His relentless efforts to bring the medicinal value of the oil to the public attention are what has made all of this possible. In many of Rick Simpson's texts/videos he stresses to make the oil yourself to be sure of quality control and we agree we with this! Our vision is that one day everybody will be able to grow their own medicine, but until then we would like to offer as much assistance to those in need as we possibly can.

Quality Control

Our growers have many years of experience flowering, harvesting and curing medical cannabis as well as extracting the oil and producing all the other medicinal products.

So customers are more informed of the extraction method and quality of oil we produce we will soon update the blog with a picture gallery detailing this procedure.

We are very open to an independent HPLC (High-performance Liquid Chromatography) test of our oil allowing us to provide exact scientific results of the medicinal value. This has proved hard to find but please do get in contact if you might be able to help.

The buds we sell are grown alongside the buds we grow specially for our oil meaning all strains of bud can be considered of relatively equal quality (except for particular strain differences). If you know an experienced cannabis user it might be a good idea to order a small quantity of bud or oil and ask them for a potency/effects review and then compare this to what Rick Simpson describes as the optimal oil effects one should observe.
